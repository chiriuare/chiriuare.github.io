The BCN Experience

Interactive video project at La Salle BCN

The BCN Experience is a project developed by four students of la Salle URL : Marina Gavin, Maria Villuendas, Jaume Capdevila and Laia Lleopart.

The idea was to create an interactive video in which the user could feel like if he or she was really visiting Barcelona. In order to achieve this goal we shot the whole video in first person narrative without revealing too much specific information about her to make it easier for everybody to feel identified. We also added the interactive part in a way that lets the user decide in six different instances in the video where to go next thus giving the sensation of making up his or her own sightseeing tour.

We filmed the project using DSLR, we edited and added the animations with Premiere and After Effects, and finally we programmed the whole experience using HTML,CSS and Javascript.
