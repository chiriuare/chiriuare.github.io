Hand Calculator

Leap Motion Project at La Salle BCN

HandCalculator is an app developed with leapmotion technology. HandCalculator is a fun way to learn the numbers of american sign language while studying math.

This project was developed in 2016 by four students from la Salle BCN : Marina Gavin, Cristina Panadès, Jordi Roig and me, Laia Lleopart We also produced, filmed and edited the video demonstration.

In this project we worked primarily with Processing and Leap Motion for coding, 3Ds Max for the 3d models of the signs and Premiere Pro and DSLR for the explainer video.
