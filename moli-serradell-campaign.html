El Molí serradell

An audiovisual campaign for the Molí Serradell camping

Advertising campaign made for Molí Serradell, a camping located near Campdevànol, known internationally for its nature-oriented mindset.

The main objectives of this campaign were to create engaging materials to show the public all that the camping has to offer. An amazing nature surroundings, the feeling of calmness, traditional cooking, and various experiences that the visitors could have.

Soon after the campaign was made public the camping also remaked his website following the branding designed for said campaign.You can check it here.

The project was made using DSLR for filming and photographing, a DIY motorized slider designed and made by myself, and finally to edit the final materials Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro and After Effects.
