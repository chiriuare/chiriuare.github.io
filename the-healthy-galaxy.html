THE HEALTHY GALAXY

A webseries promoting healthy habits among kids featuring Pau and Marc Gasol

This webseries from Gasol Foundation starring Pau and Marc Gasol, focuses on promoting healthy habits among children. The show has 5 episodes, each of one focusing on one key point: Sport & physical activity, healthy diet, rest and emotional well-being.

In this project I worked as a Motion designer and Motion Grapher, working entirely with Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere, Audition, Media Encoder and DaVinci Resolve. I also created promotional images and GIFs to advertise the project on different platforms, as well as other informative images.
